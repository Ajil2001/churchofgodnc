ABOUT US


Welcome to Church of God in Raleigh


At Church of God in Raleigh , we are a welcoming community dedicated to spiritual growth, fellowship, and service. Our ministries cater to individuals and families of all ages, offering opportunities to deepen your faith and connect with others.
We provide Sunday School classes, led by passionate teachers who share the Word of God, along with dedicated ministries such as Women’s Ministry, and Youth Ministry, to support and encourage every member of our congregation.
You are not just a visitor here you are family. As you explore our website, we pray that the Holy Spirit uplifts you and fills your heart with peace and blessings.
We invite you to join us in worship, share your prayer requests, and experience the love and grace of God. We look forward to connecting with you soon!
